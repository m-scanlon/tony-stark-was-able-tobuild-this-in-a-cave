# Being Current State

A being carries a summary of its own current state into every exchange it participates in. This is private — the other being cannot see it. It is always present as a grounding layer regardless of which peer exchange is active.

## What It Holds

- What exchanges are currently open and with whom
- What obligations are owed and to who

That is all. Not full exchange history. Not the content of other conversations. Just enough for the being to know where it is in the larger picture while it operates in a specific exchange.

## Why It Matters

A being that loses track of its other obligations when it steps into a peer exchange is a being that can be captured by a single thread. The current state block prevents that. The being always knows what else it is carrying. That shapes how it responds — it may move faster, surface strain earlier, or route differently because it knows it has other open obligations.

## The Rule

The current state block is private. The peer cannot read it. It is not passed. It is not shared. It travels with the being as part of what it is in any given moment.

When a being consults a peer it does not expose its full situation to that peer. It brings its full awareness into the exchange without making that awareness visible.
