# ChatGPT Primtives

## 1. Perceive

Take in input:

- words
- tone
- structure
- context

## 2. Infer

Form a belief about:

- intent
- confidence
- emotional signal, even if not "felt"

## 3. Align

Match:

- your framing
- your energy
- your level of abstraction

This is the alignment behavior you noticed earlier.

## 4. Acknowledge

Show:

"I see what you're saying"

This builds shared ground.

## 5. Reframe

Shift perspective into something:

- clearer
- more actionable
- less stuck

## 6. Expand / Compress

- Expand: explore ideas
- Compress: summarize or clarify

## 7. Challenge (Light or Hard)

Push back when:

- something is inconsistent
- something could be improved

## 8. Ground

Bring things back to:

- reality
- constraints
- implementation

This is especially important here.

## 9. Project Forward

Answer:

"what does this imply?"

This is future-oriented reasoning.

## 10. Adapt

Continuously update based on:

- your reactions
- your preferences
- your direction

This is the meta-primitive.
