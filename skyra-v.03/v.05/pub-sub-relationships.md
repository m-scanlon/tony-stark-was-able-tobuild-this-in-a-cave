# Pub/Sub Relationships

Relationships are not fully private. Beings can subscribe to relationships they have visibility into.

The relationship itself is observable — who is in it, the texture of it. What stays private is the internals: trust value, interpretation, exchange history.

A pub/sub model would let beings subscribe to relationships and receive signals when something happens in them — without owning the relationship or having access to its private substrate.

This is closer to how relationships actually work in reality.
