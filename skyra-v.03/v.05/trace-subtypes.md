# Trace Subtypes — Intent And Resolution

Trace already means a retained factual happening. Opening an exchange with intent is a happening. Closing it with a resolution is a happening. They are typed traces.

## The Retained Artifact Family Stays Flat

- `trace` — what happened
- `understanding` — what it meant
- `salience` — what carried weight
- `tension` — what remained unresolved

No new top-level members. Trace gets subtypes.

## Trace Subtypes

**trace.intent** — formed when an exchange opens. The being's declared purpose for the exchange. What it is trying to resolve. Everything that happens inside the exchange is measured against it. Private to the opener — the peer knows something is wanted from them but does not see the intent behind it.

**trace.resolution** — formed when the opener closes the exchange. The judgment that the intent has been met. Carries what was produced — the answer, the decision, the work. This is the artifact passed back up the chain as payment of the relational debt.

## Why Subtypes Not New Members

Intent and resolution are exchange-scoped trace events. They are factual happenings with a specific position in the exchange lifecycle. They do not carry interpretation, weight, or unresolved tension on their own. Trace is the right family. The subtype carries the specificity.
