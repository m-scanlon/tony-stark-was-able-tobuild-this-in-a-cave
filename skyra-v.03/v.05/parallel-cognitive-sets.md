# Parallel Cognitive Sets

Prefrontal has the ability to duplicate its inner cognitive beings when it judges that two tasks it is currently handling have no overlap.

This is not repair differentiation — the beings aren't broken or overloaded. It's load differentiation. The tasks are genuinely independent and can be run in parallel without shared state contaminating either thread.

Each duplicate set is fully equipped — strategy, values, consequence, conflict, theory-of-mind. The two threads run independently. When both resolve, prefrontal synthesizes or closes them independently depending on whether the results need to be reconciled.

## How it differs from repair differentiation

Repair differentiation fires when a being boundary is wrong and repeated failure has made that visible. The trigger is pattern over time.

Parallel cognitive sets fire when prefrontal makes a judgment call that two active tasks are orthogonal enough to warrant isolation. The trigger is a present-moment assessment of task overlap.

Same mechanism — being factory, same creation paths. Different trigger and different lifetime. Parallel cognitive sets are probably temporary. They exist for the duration of the tasks and dissolve when both threads close.

## Open questions

- How does prefrontal assess overlap — is that a judgment call through inference or a structural rule?
- Do the duplicate beings share any state at all or are they fully isolated?
- When both threads close, does prefrontal synthesize the results or just route them independently outward?
- What happens if the tasks turn out to have overlap mid-execution — do the threads merge, or does prefrontal hold both and reconcile at close?
- Are the duplicate beings differentiated beings in the formal sense, or a lighter-weight temporary construct?
