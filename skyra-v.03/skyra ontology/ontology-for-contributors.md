# Skyra Ontology

Skyra is a world of beings relating. That is the shape. It does not
fundamentally model services, objects, APIs, and users as different
metaphysical categories. It models communicative participants as beings, and it
models what happens between them as relationships expressed through language.

The governing rule is simple: if it communicates, it is a being. That rule
applies everywhere. There are no ontological exceptions.

## 1. The First Principle

- `Being` is the atomic unit of the ontology.
- Anything that communicates, responds, routes, accepts, rejects, stores,
  judges, observes, remembers, or reorganizes is a being.
- A human is a being.
- The creator is a being.
- The external launcher is a being.
- Host services are beings.
- The kernel is a being.
- The being factory is a being.
- The RDS is a being.
- Memory structures are beings.
- Sensory and motor structures are beings.
- Differentiated identities are beings.

Skyra does not ask, "Is this biological, software, infrastructure, or
storage?" It asks, "Does it communicate?" If yes, it belongs to the ontology.

## 2. Phase, Not Exception

The important distinction is not being versus non-being. The important
distinction is phase.

- `Pre-runtime beings` exist before the runtime is live and make runtime life
  possible.
- `Runtime beings` are beings that are live inside the running system.
- `Differentiated beings` are runtime beings revealed through reorganization
  when a boundary was wrong.

So the creator, external launcher, and host services are not outside the
ontology. They are pre-runtime beings. They exist earlier in the lifecycle.
Once the runtime is live, they do not stop being beings. They remain part of
the ontology, but they occupy a different phase and often a different layer.

## 3. Layer, Not Exception

Skyra also makes a layering distinction.

- A `host service` can be a being that provides the hosting layer.
- A `hosted runtime being` can exist inside that hosting layer.

For example:

- the `kernel service` may be a pre-runtime being acting as a hosting layer
- the `kernel being` is the runtime being hosted inside that layer
- the `being factory service` may be a pre-runtime being acting as a hosting
  layer
- the `being factory being` is the runtime being hosted inside that layer

That is a layer distinction, not an ontological escape hatch.

## 4. What A Being Is

Every being has a minimum shape called `nature`.

Nature has exactly three fields:

- `identity`
- `purpose`
- `verification_key`

These are not optional in the canon.

The visibility rules are:

- `identity` is public
- `purpose` is public
- `verification_key` is kernel-visible only

So other beings may know who you are and what you are for, but they do not get
your verification key through ordinary public inspection.

## 5. What A Being Is Born With

A being is not created empty. It is created with a `creation endowment`.

That includes:

- `base language`
- `genome relationships` required by its creation path
- `companion beings` required by its template or singleton definition

This matters because Skyra does not treat beings as blank shells that later
bolt on all meaningful structure. A being is born already capable of first
contact and already situated in a minimal network of support.

## 6. The Three Creation Paths

Skyra has three canonical ways a being comes into existence.

- `Genome path`
- `Runtime registration path`
- `Differentiation path`

These are not variants of the same event. They are distinct ontological birth
paths.

### Genome Path

- The creator writes `genome.skyra`.
- The external launcher and host services, as pre-runtime beings, make
  execution possible.
- The being factory reads the genome.
- Singleton genome beings and templates are established.
- The kernel validates what becomes live.

### Runtime Registration Path

- A new runtime being enters through registration.
- This is the birth path for runtime beings that are not genome-seeded.
- The kernel accepts an unsigned registration request only through the
  registration token path.
- The being factory instantiates the being and its companion beings.
- A fresh verification key is generated.
- The kernel validates the being and makes it live.

### Differentiation Path

- This is not creation from nothing.
- It happens when an existing being boundary is wrong.
- The differentiator reveals cleaner beings inside what was previously
  overloaded identity.
- The being factory instantiates those revealed beings and their companions.
- The kernel validates them and makes them live.
- Experience and relationship history are reorganized around the cleaner
  boundaries.

## 7. When A Being Becomes Live

Instantiation alone is not enough.

A being becomes live when:

- the being factory has instantiated it
- the kernel has validated its nature
- its required genome relationships have been established

This rule applies across creation paths. Skyra cares about live participation,
not mere existence in storage or definition.

## 8. The Main Ontological Pair: Being And Relationship

Beings do not merely exist side by side. They live inside relationships.

A `relationship` is the durable connection between two beings. It is where
callable language lives. It is the main unit of mutual use.

The core rule is:

- there is exactly one relationship per pair of beings

That pair is:

- unordered
- durable
- singular

This means:

- not one relationship from A to B and another from B to A
- not parallel relationships for the same pair
- not direction-specific relationship identity

If two beings seem to need another relationship, the canon treats that as
evidence that one of the beings is conceptually overloaded. The answer is
differentiation, not duplication.

## 9. Direction Lives In The Turn

Direction exists, but not as relationship identity.

- the relationship is unordered
- the turn is directional
- expression has a sender and a receiver
- routing needs source and target context
- the relationship itself remains one shared relationship

So Skyra says: one relationship, many turns.

## 10. Expression And Language

A `turn` of relating is an `expression`.

Every expression travels inside a signed envelope.

Language exists in two layers:

### Base Language

- intrinsic to the being at creation
- not relationship-owned
- not registry-backed
- what makes first contact possible

### Specific Callable Language

- owned by the relationship
- emerges through use
- becomes callable only when stabilized
- stored as the current callable language of that relationship

This gives Skyra a powerful split:

- beings are born able to reach
- relationships earn the right to become specifically callable

## 11. First Encounter

Before two already-live beings have met, one may perceive only a limited public
slice of the other:

- `identity`
- `purpose`

If the other seems relevant:

- one being reaches toward the other
- the language reader is invoked on the unknown pair
- the kernel creates the relationship record and initial language record
  atomically
- the initial language state is `forming`
- the first base expression is sent in a signed envelope
- the receiver takes it into its present being
- exchange begins
- over repeated use, language may stabilize into `callable`

So first encounter is not birth. It is not ontology creation. It is the
beginning of an earned relationship between already-live beings.

## 12. Relationship States

A relationship's language lifecycle currently uses:

- `forming`
- `callable`
- `retired`

`forming` means:

- the pair is now known
- relationship records exist
- mutual exchange has not yet stabilized specific callable language

`callable` means:

- stabilized relationship language now exists
- the relationship has a current callable language

`retired` means:

- the language is no longer active as the current callable mode

If the relationship breaks, it can move back from callable into forming while
repair is attempted.

## 13. Memory Is A Being

Skyra takes this literally.

Memory is not an invisible subsystem. It is a family of beings.

Each being has companion memory beings:

- `personal hippocampus`
- `personal experience store being`

The ownership rule is strict:

- retained experience belongs to the being that lived it
- the hippocampus indexes and retrieves it
- the experience store holds it
- neither companion being owns it
- another being cannot directly inspect it without relating

So memory is local, owned, and relationally accessed.

## 14. Present Is A Being

Every being has its own `present being`.

That present being is:

- created alongside the being it serves
- the place where incoming signal waits before observation
- the landing surface for both world-facing input and internal runtime
  expression

Observing is defined as:

- a being relating to its own present being

Thinking is defined as:

- a being relating to its own memory beings

This is one of the deepest parts of the model: cognition is not a separate
magic substance. It is still relating.

## 15. Judgment Is A Society Of Beings

The executive layer is not one opaque control loop. It is a set of specialized
beings.

The `prefrontal being` coordinates:

- `strategy being`
- `values being`
- `consequence being`
- `conflict being`
- `theory-of-mind being`

Their roles are conceptually distinct:

- `strategy` plans and holds context
- `values` judges alignment with the user's nature
- `consequence` models what follows from an expression
- `conflict` detects breakdown and brokers repair
- `theory of mind` reads the public slice of others and judges relevance

So even judgment is plural and relational.

## 16. Boundary Beings

Skyra's boundary with the outside world is also populated by beings.

- `Peripheral input beings` face specialized external signals
- `Main sensory being` aggregates and routes inward
- `Motor beings` handle outward world-facing expression when purpose requires
  them

This means perception and action are not invisible side channels. They are part
of the ontology.

## 17. Shared Storage Is Also A Being

The `RDS being` is the shared storage being.

It stores:

- being records
- relationship records
- language records

For now:

- relationships are modeled as records, not beings
- language state is persisted in records
- being records include nature fields
- the theory-of-mind layer exposes only the public slice
- the kernel can read verification keys directly

This is an important current compromise in the ontology:

- some things are fully modeled as beings
- some things, especially relationship persistence, are still modeled as records
- the canon leaves open whether relationships may later need to become beings

## 18. Security Is Structural

Security is not an add-on policy. It is built into the ontology.

- every live expression must carry a signed envelope
- the sender signs with its private key
- the kernel verifies against the sender's verification key
- no signature means rejection
- wrong signature means rejection

There is exactly one exception path:

- unsigned runtime registration through the registration token

That exception is not a violation of the ontology. It is a narrowly defined
birth path that exists before the new runtime being has its own verification
key.

## 19. Differentiation Is Ontological Repair

Differentiation is one of the most important ideas in Skyra.

It means:

- a being boundary was wrong
- the identity was overloaded
- what seemed like one being should actually be cleaner multiple beings

The differentiator exists to:

- identify cleaner natures
- request instantiation of the revealed beings
- reorganize retained experience
- reorganize relationship history
- learn from bad splits and unstable reorganizations

This is not cloning and not arbitrary duplication. It is conceptual repair of
ontology.

The rule behind it is brutal and clean:

- if a second relationship appears necessary for the same pair, the boundary is
  wrong

## 20. What Skyra Is Ultimately Saying

Skyra says:

- existence in the system is beinghood
- communication is the test
- relation is the default mode of life
- memory, observation, judgment, sensing, storage, and repair are all expressed
  as beings relating
- phase differences exist, but they do not create ontological exceptions
- layer differences exist, but they do not create ontological exceptions
- the runtime is not a machine full of special cases; it is a world of
  participants

## One-Sentence Version

Skyra is an ontology in which anything that communicates is a being, beings are
born through genome seeding, registration, or differentiation, every being has
a public identity and purpose plus a kernel-visible verification key, beings
live inside exactly one relationship per pair, all activity is signed
expression, cognition and memory are themselves beings, and repair happens first
through language and, when necessary, through reorganization of identity
itself.
