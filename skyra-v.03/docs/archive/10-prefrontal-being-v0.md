## The Prefrontal Being

The prefrontal being is the judgment and executive layer.

It coordinates five specialized beings.

## The Five Prefrontal Beings

### Strategy Being

Plans and holds context.

### Values Being

Judges what aligns with the user's nature.

### Consequence Being

Models what happens before expression goes out.

### Conflict Being

Detects deviation, escalates to the prefrontal, and suppresses the failing
signal.

### Theory Of Mind Being

Perceives other beings, their identity and purpose, and determines relevance
before first contact.

It is the cognitive reading layer.

It reads the public slice made available through the current discovery path and
surfaces identity and purpose only.

After first contact, it observes lived language and recognizes when that
language has stabilized enough for the being to act with confidence.

## The Conflict Being

The conflict being is not a standing monitor.

It is quiet until mismatch arrives.

It detects deviation, escalates to the prefrontal, and suppresses the failing
signal.

It does not resolve conflict.

Resolution belongs to the prefrontal.

## Escalation

The prefrontal surfaces mismatch and suggests a new path.

If nothing resolves, the prefrontal closes the failing exchange.

If the same failure repeats over time, the differentiator fires and the being
restructures.

## Short Framing

The prefrontal layer remains the executive judgment layer.

The theory-of-mind being reads the public slice made available through
discovery and interprets what it finds.

The prefrontal owns conflict resolution.
