# Nature And Relationships v0

## Purpose

This document locks the canonical buckets in the current `v.03` model.

## Nature

Nature has exactly three fields:

- identity
- purpose
- verification_key

Identity is public.

Purpose is public.

Verification key is kernel-visible only.

Nature is locked at the creation path.

## Creation Endowment

A being is created with:

- base language
- genome relationships required by its creation path
- companion beings required by its template

This is real and important.

It is not a separate formal bucket.

## Relationships

Relationships are what beings live inside.

Two origins exist:

- genome-seeded relationships
- earned relationships formed through living

Specific callable language remains relationship-owned.

## Visibility Before First Contact

Before specific callable language exists, the visible slice is:

- identity
- purpose

Verification key is not visible through the registry reader.

## Registry Reader

The registry reader surfaces identity and purpose only.

That is the correct and complete public slice before first contact.

## Short Framing

Nature is identity, purpose, and verification key.

Relationships may be genome-seeded or earned.

Base language and companion beings belong to the creation path, not to a formal
third bucket.
