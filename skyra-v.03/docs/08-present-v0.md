# Present v0

## Purpose

This document locks `present` in the current `v.03` canon.

## Core Framing

Every being operates from its own present.

Present is the being's full operative reality at a given moment.

It always holds:

- nature
- relationships
- the active exchange

These are simultaneous.

Present is not a passive queue.

Present is not a companion container.

What is present for your being is not the same as what is present for mine.

## Delivery Path

### Outside World

Peripheral input beings receive specialized signal from the outside world.

They feed the main sensory being.

The main sensory being routes what arrives into the receiver's present.

### Internal Runtime

The kernel verifies the incoming signed envelope and moves internal expression
into the receiving being's present.

So whether something arrives from the world or elsewhere in the runtime, it
becomes part of the being's present before cognition responds.

## Observe

A being observes from its present.

## Status

The older model of present as a passive queue or container is retired.

## Future Direction

Future docs may refine how differentiated attention reshapes present without
reintroducing the older container model.

## Short Framing

Present is the being's operative reality:

- nature
- relationships
- the active exchange

World-facing input and internal runtime expression both become part of present
before response.
