# Being Shape v0

## Purpose

This document locks the minimum shape of a being in the current `v.03` model.

It also locks how beings become live on each creation path.

## Minimum Shape

The minimum shape of a being is its nature:

- identity
- purpose
- verification_key

Identity is public.

Purpose is public and unique to the being.

Verification key is kernel-visible only.

Nature is locked at the creation path.

## Creation Paths

A being may come into existence through:

- genome seeding
- runtime registration
- differentiation

Genome beings are seeded from `genome.skyra`.

Runtime beings are born through registration.

Differentiated beings are locked when the differentiation path completes.

## Creation Endowment

Every being is created with:

- base language
- the genome relationships required by its creation path
- companion beings required by its template or singleton definition

Those companion beings include:

- personal hippocampus
- personal experience store being
- present being

## Live Status

A being becomes live when:

1. the being factory has instantiated it
2. the kernel has validated its nature
3. its genome relationships have been established

That rule applies to runtime beings and differentiated beings.

For genome beings, the same factory and kernel handoff happens during genome
bootstrap after the external launcher seeds the being factory.

## Consequences

This means:

- registration is not universal being birth
- registration is the birth path for runtime beings
- the kernel does not invent beings
- the being factory does not validate beings
- no live being exists without a verification key

## Short Framing

A being minimally has identity, purpose, and a kernel-visible verification key.

It becomes live when the being factory instantiates it and the kernel validates
it on the relevant creation path.
