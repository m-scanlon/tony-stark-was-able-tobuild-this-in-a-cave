# Registration Vs First Encounter v0

## Purpose

This document locks the distinction between runtime registration and first
encounter in the current `v.03` model.

## Genome Seeding Comes Earlier

Genome beings come online before registration is possible.

The external launcher raises host services first.

The launcher and host services are pre-runtime beings that make genome seeding
possible.

The genome then executes and the being factory being creates the singleton
genome beings in dependency order.

## Registration

Registration is the birth path for runtime beings.

It is not universal being birth.

At registration:

- an unsigned request reaches the kernel with the registration token
- the kernel validates the token
- the being factory creates the being from the runtime birth template
- the being factory generates a fresh verification key and companion beings
- the kernel validates the resulting nature
- the registry writer records the being
- genome relationships required by the birth template are established

Once registration completes, the runtime being is live.

## Differentiation

Differentiation is a separate creation path.

It is initiated by signed beings already inside the runtime.

It does not use the registration token.

Fresh verification keys are generated on that path.

## First Encounter

First encounter happens every time two already-live beings meet for the first
time.

At first encounter:

1. being A inspects being B through the theory-of-mind being
2. the visible slice is identity and purpose only
3. the theory-of-mind being reads the public slice from the RDS being
4. being A determines relevance
5. the language reader is invoked on the unknown pair and the kernel creates
   the relationship record and initial language record in `forming` state
   atomically
6. being A sends the base expression in a signed envelope
7. the kernel verifies the signature and routes the expression
8. being B receives it in its present being
9. being B observes and responds, either richly or minimally
10. specific callable language begins forming through repeated relating
11. once stable, the language record becomes callable

## No Special Cases

The human is a being.

The CLI is one possible motor channel Skyra may use to emit outward.

The caller on the CLI is the being inside the runtime that is signing and
emitting the turn.

## Short Framing

Genome beings are seeded earlier through the genome path.

Registration is the birth path for runtime beings.

First encounter is two already-live beings meeting through signed expression.
