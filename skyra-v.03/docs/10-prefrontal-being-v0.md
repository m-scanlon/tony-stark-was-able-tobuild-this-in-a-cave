# Prefrontal Being v0

## Purpose

This document locks the prefrontal layer in the current `v.03` canon.

## The Prefrontal Being

The prefrontal being is the judgment and executive layer.

It coordinates five specialized beings.

## The Five Prefrontal Beings

### Strategy Being

Plans and holds context.

### Values Being

Judges what aligns with the user's nature.

### Consequence Being

Models what happens before expression goes out.

### Conflict Being

Detects conflict pre-expression and brokers renegotiation post-expression.

### Theory Of Mind Being

Perceives other beings, their identity and purpose, and determines relevance
before first contact.

It is the cognitive reading layer.

It reads from the RDS being through their genome relationship and surfaces
identity and purpose only.

After first contact, it observes forming language and recognizes when that
language has stabilized enough to mark the language record callable.

## The Conflict Being

The conflict being detects breakdown, brokers repair by default, and escalates
identity problems to the differentiator.

It does not author the repair itself.

## Escalation

When renegotiation keeps failing in a pattern that retained experience
recognizes as an identity problem, the conflict being relates to the
differentiator and hands off.

That judgment improves through use.

## Short Framing

The prefrontal layer remains the executive judgment layer.

The theory-of-mind being reads from the RDS being and interprets what it finds.

The conflict being repairs first and escalates identity problems when needed.
