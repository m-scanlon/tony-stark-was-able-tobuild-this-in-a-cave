# Strain v0

## Purpose

This document locks `strain` as a first-class primitive in the current `v.03`
model.

It defines what `strain` means, who emits it, and how it differs from
`conflict`.

## Core Rule

`strain` is pre-failure load.

It is present when a being is still holding its current interpretation
coherently, but holding that coherence is getting expensive.

`strain` comes before explicit mismatch.

It is earlier than `conflict`.

## What Strain Is Not

`strain` is not:

- trust
- confidence
- explicit conflict
- a named gap
- a kernel metric

It is the rising cost of not breaking yet.

## Authorship

`strain` is self-reported by the being paying the cost.

It is emitted when that being notices holding-cost rise in its own present
moment.

It is not stamped by the kernel.

It is not inferred by another being.

## Local First

`strain` is private by default.

A being may experience `strain` without expressing it outward.

That private signal is enough for local coherence maintenance to respond.

## Expression Surface

When a being chooses to express `strain` outward, it travels as an optional
integer field on expression.

It is a first-class language flag.

Outward `strain` is not mandatory telemetry.

It is chosen disclosure.

## Responders

The hippocampus listens for `strain`.

Its role is to reinstate fit before failure when possible.

`conflict` is the later signal.

The conflict being listens for `conflict` after mismatch has already become
explicit.

So the runtime arc is:

- `strain` -> coherence maintenance before break
- `conflict` -> repair after break

## Relation And Vulnerability

When a being expresses `strain` outward, it is making itself vulnerable.

It is saying that it is still holding, but holding is costly.

That changes the live frame immediately.

It does not mechanically update trust in that moment.

Later, the way that expressed `strain` was met may become retained experience,
and trust may move through that retained consequence.

## Short Framing

`strain` is the first-class signal of rising coherence cost before explicit
failure.
