# Strain v0

## Purpose

This document locks `strain` as a first-class primitive in the current `v.03`
model.

It defines what `strain` means, who carries it, how it surfaces outward, and
how it differs from `conflict`.

## Core Rule

`strain` is pre-failure load.

It is present when a being is still holding its current interpretation
coherently, but holding that coherence is getting expensive.

`strain` comes before explicit mismatch.

It is earlier than `conflict`.

## What Strain Is Not

`strain` is not:

- trust
- confidence
- explicit conflict
- a named gap
- a kernel metric

It is the rising cost of not breaking yet.

## Authorship

`strain` is self-reported by the being paying the cost.

It is emitted when that being notices holding-cost rise in its own present
moment.

It is not stamped by the kernel.

It is not inferred by another being.

## Local First

`strain` is private by default.

A being may experience `strain` without expressing it outward.

That private signal is enough for local coherence maintenance to respond.

## Expression Surface

`strain` does not travel outward as `strain`.

When a being chooses to surface `strain` outward, it becomes `stress` or
`anger` on expression.

That outward surfacing is not mandatory telemetry.

It is chosen disclosure.

`stress` carries surfaced load and reach.

`anger` carries surfaced escalation.

The canonical outward ladder is locked in
[22-conflict-and-emotional-routing-v0.md](/Users/mikepersonal/tony-stark-was-able-tobuild-this-in-a-cave/skyra-v.03/docs/22-conflict-and-emotional-routing-v0.md).

## Responders

The hippocampus listens for `strain`.

Its role is to reinstate fit before failure when possible while the load is
still internal.

`conflict` is the later signal.

It marks explicit mismatch after repair failed and the exchange was closed.

So the runtime arc is:

- internal `strain` -> surfaced `stress` or `anger`
- `anger` above threshold -> prefrontal routing copy
- repair attempt -> explicit `conflict` if repair fails and the exchange closes
- repeated failure over time -> differentiation

See
[22-conflict-and-emotional-routing-v0.md](/Users/mikepersonal/tony-stark-was-able-tobuild-this-in-a-cave/skyra-v.03/docs/22-conflict-and-emotional-routing-v0.md).

## Relation And Vulnerability

When a being surfaces `strain` outward as `stress` or `anger`, it is making
itself vulnerable.

It is saying that it is still holding, but holding is costly.

That changes the present immediately.

It does not mechanically update trust in that moment.

Later, the way that surfaced load was met may become retained experience, and
trust may move through that retained consequence.

## Short Framing

`strain` is the first-class internal signal of rising coherence cost before
explicit failure.

When it surfaces outward, it becomes `stress` or `anger`.
