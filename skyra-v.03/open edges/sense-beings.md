# Sense Being

When an external signal arrives from a being that has no existing relationship in the runtime, something has to decide whether it matters.

The sense being is a singleton that lives permanently at the external boundary. It owns all external exchanges — one exchange per external contact, the same way prefrontal holds separate exchanges with each of its peers. That's what gives it cross-thread context: it's already holding everything happening at the boundary simultaneously.

Its job on a new contact is a single binary: pass it forward or don't.

If it passes forward, the signal enters the inward walk. If it doesn't, the signal dies at the boundary without disturbing anything internal.

Because it's singleton and persistent, the sense being is also the edge being for every external exchange. When michael talks to Skyra, the sense being owns that exchange. When an email arrives, the sense being owns that exchange too. Separate exchanges, one being.

## What makes this different from sensory

Sensory is a fixed relay. It receives and routes inward without interpretation.

The sense being holds all external threads simultaneously and makes a judgment call across them. It's the difference between a switchboard and attention.

## Open questions

- Is the sense being cognitive (reasons through inference) or does it use a simpler structural rule?
- If cognitive: its present includes all live external exchanges. How does that render without becoming overwhelming?
- If it passes a new contact forward, does it also seed the relationship between that external being and the runtime? Or does that happen downstream?
- Can the sense being be wrong — pass something through that shouldn't have made it, or drop something that mattered? What's the correction path?
- How does the sense being relate to the existing sensory being — does it replace it, sit alongside it, or absorb it?

## Relationship to existing open edges

This pairs with external-exchange-in-present: the sense being is the natural owner of the external exchange record that travels inward with every signal.

It also pairs with being-types: the sense being is probably its own type — singleton, boundary-resident, multi-exchange.
